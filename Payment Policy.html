Payment Privacy

Method 1: Payment upon receipt (COD)

Method 2: Pay via online banking (Transfer)

II. Ensure transaction safety

Buyers should provide complete information (name, address, phone number, email) when purchasing from SPORT SHOP so that they can quickly contact SPORT SHOP in case an error occurs.

In the case of a home delivery transaction, the buyer should only pay after inspecting the goods in detail and being satisfied with the product.

When paying online with domestic ATM cards, Visa, Master, buyers should do it themselves and not reveal card information. SPORT SHOP does not store the buyer's card information after payment, but through the affiliated bank's system. Card information should be absolutely confidential for customers.

In case an error occurs during the online payment process, SPORT SHOP will be the unit to resolve the issue for the customer within 1 working day of receiving information from the person making the transaction.

Warranty Policy

+ Commitment to standard goods as described, images, videos.

1. Warranty scope

1.1. Products and warranty period

The product warranty period is confirmed based on the warranty card accompanying each SPORT SHOP product and the purchase invoice but must not exceed the warranty period from the date of manufacture. In case the Customer loses the warranty card and purchase invoice, the warranty period from the date of manufacture will apply.

Warranty period: See detailed product information on the website or warranty card included with the product.

1.2. Valid warranty conditions

The product will be warranted if it fully meets the following conditions:

· The product is still under warranty at the time the Customer requests, applied as section 1.1

· Damage due to product quality or technical errors during production.

· The product must be in its original form, product label, serial number (S/N)

· The product is not subject to warranty denial as specified in Article 1.3

1.3. In case of warranty refusal

· Products damaged by natural disasters, fires, floods, lightning strikes...

· The product is placed in a dusty or damp place;

· The product is deformed due to heat or external force;

· Product warranty expires.

2.1. Basic warranty services

· Warranty repair service

When the product is damaged, customers can bring the product directly to the SPORT SHOP Warranty Center:

To request warranty service. At the same time, the customer will be asked by the center to provide information, including:

√ Product information: Model, material, color, date of purchase...

√ Customer Information: Customer name, contact phone number, contact address and repair service request content.

Out-of-warranty repair service

In case the Customer needs to request repair services outside the warranty, SPORT SHOP Warranty Centers:

Responsible for serving and repairing products enthusiastically and thoughtfully. Customers will pay costs incurred for repairs such as replacement parts, wages and other costs (if any) based on the agreement between the two parties.
